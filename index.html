Accessibility isn't that hard.

It's time to build applications everyone can use. It's time for applications to be 'born accessible' instead of having accessibility added as an afterthought.

Why it's important.

Don't think about accessibility much? Then you're not one of the almost 60 MILLION Americans that live with a disability on a daily basis.

Why should I care?

This isn't about doing favors. This is about giving everyone the opportunity to access content and services. Oh, and it's most likely THE LAW where you work.

But I'm not disabled!

So? Someone you know likely is. And you didn't even notice because of some sort of accessibility aid they didn't tell you about.
